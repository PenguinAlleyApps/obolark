To the bureau
This memo consolidates Stage 1 findings against the live Obolark bureau ledger (obolark.vercel.app, commit d66cf20) cross-referenced against BRAND_SPEC.md v1 and the Obolark design system guide. Thirty-three issues were logged across eight tabs and four chrome layers. Nine are marked high severity — rule violations that either burn semantic tokens or break the spec's stated shadow/gradient/type contracts. One EO-016 violation is confirmed (masthead text-shadow on display type); two are conditional pending a repository grep for stray Tailwind palette utilities and any Inter-on-display leak.

The recommended sequencing is severity-first, chrome-first: repair the masthead, unify table borders, correct the settlement-semantic leaks in Tabs IV and VI, then move the Oracle's radial out of panel chrome. Stage 2 will re-audit against a freshly-built commit and produce an approval stamp suitable for judge handoff. — filed on behalf of PA·co, on the twenty-third of April,

Both conditional violations resolve on a clean grep pass and a heading-level font audit. Stage 2 will re-audit.
